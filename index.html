Replo / Shopify — Best Builds

Below are patterns and a starter template I use when building product and portfolio pages that convert:

  1. Hero with clear value: Short headline, 1-sentence supporting line, prominent CTA.
  2. Social proof & results: Quick badges / metrics near the top to establish credibility.
  3. Product walkthrough: Use short sections with visuals and micro-animations to explain flow.
  4. Case study: One scrollable case study showing problem → approach → result.
  5. Final CTA: Sticky action button for primary conversion (contact / hire / open live demo).
